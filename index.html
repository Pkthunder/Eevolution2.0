How To Play

Step One: Choose A Pokemon!

Both players must first choose which Pokemon they would like you use. To do this, click on the Pokemon you wish to select, or select the random button at the top of the screen. An example is given below


Step Two: Battle!

Once both players have selected their Pokemon, the move buttons will become activated. Both players must select which move they wish to use, then the turn will play out. The Text Display will record everything that happens and can be referred to at anytime. There is a complete Move List that will explain what every move does that can also be referred to at anytme. There is also an option to toggle tooltips (mouse-over triggered move descriptions) on and off.


Quick Overview of the Player Panel

Note: to all hardcore Pokemon fans, some moves may have been adjusted slightly in an attempt to balance the game. You may want to quickly review each move before playing, because they may be different compared to the actual Pokemon games.

Just like in the real Pokemon games, the battle will continue until one Pokemon faints. Have fun!
